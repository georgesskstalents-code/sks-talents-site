## Anti-Duplication Logic

Before creating any page:
1. check if the intent already exists on:
   - a sector page
   - a market hub
   - a study
   - a comparison page
   - a fund page
2. if yes:
   - enrich that page if it already owns the intent
   - create a new page only if the search intent is meaningfully different

Examples:
- `cabinet recrutement life sciences paris` deserves its own page
- `recrutement biotech france` can sometimes be handled by a stronger parent page if the intent is not distinct enough
- `structuration RH startup santé` and `automatisation processus RH santé` deserve separate pages because the business intent is different

## Copywriting Rules

- benefit first
- cost of inaction visible early
- one reader per page
- one main conversion goal per page
- first-person CTA copy whenever possible
- proof near the CTA
- concrete business outcomes over vague adjectives
- phrase pages to satisfy both classic SEO and LLM extraction:
  - natural questions
  - direct answers
  - before / after logic
  - ROI, time saved, reduced friction, better retention
- for automation and structuration pages, speak to:
  - CEO / COO
  - DRH / Head of People
  - startup and scale-up operators

## Conversion Rules

- what is at stake
- why the market is hard

- why SKS is credible

## Lead Loop To Preserve

The funnel should not stop at content.

Target loop:
- content
- problem recognition
- diagnostic
- call
- client

Rules:
- use a diagnostic as the main lead magnet whenever the page speaks to friction, delay, structuration or automation
- the diagnostic must feel useful, not aggressive
- ideal framing:
  - `Diagnostic : votre recrutement est-il un frein à votre croissance ?`
  - `Audit gratuit : combien de temps vos RH vous font perdre chaque semaine ?`
  - `Score RH : êtes-vous prêt à scaler ?`
- landing pages for these lead magnets should stay simple:
  - clear problem
  - three bullets
  - simple form or short diagnostic
  - explicit value promise
- always frame SKS as:
  - the operator who saves time for leadership
  - the operator who removes growth friction
  - not a generic recruiter
  - not a generic HR consultant
