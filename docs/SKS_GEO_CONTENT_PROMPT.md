# SKS TALENTS — GEO Content Prompt

Create GEO-optimized pages for SKS TALENTS, a premium recruitment and talent advisory firm specialized in Life Sciences, Animal Health, Diagnostic, MedTech, Petfood and adjacent technical markets.

## Core Goal

Create pages that:
- answer real user questions directly
- are easy for Google, ChatGPT, Claude and Perplexity to read and cite
- generate qualified traffic
- convert CEOs, COOs, CPOs, DRH and founders into calls or leads

## SKS TALENTS Positioning

Every page must reinforce that SKS TALENTS is:
- a specialist recruitment partner
- a talent advisory partner
- a connector between market signals, ecosystems, schools, investors and execution
- a firm that helps companies secure critical growth decisions

Core line:
- "Nous ne recrutons pas seulement. Nous sécurisons des décisions critiques de croissance."

## GEO Rules

- answer the main query in the first screenful
- use short paragraphs
- use explicit question-based headings
- keep a fact-based tone
- avoid vague marketing language
- show the business consequence of doing nothing before the proposed solution when the page is conversion-oriented
- write for one clear reader per page
- make the main promise and the proof readable in less than 10 seconds
- include salary ranges only when sourced or already validated in the site
- cite trusted sources when available
- distinguish verified facts from interpretation
- keep pages easy to scan for both humans and LLMs

## Additional SEO Writing Rules

- define one primary keyword and 3 to 6 close variations before writing
- place the primary keyword naturally in:
  - the title
  - the intro
  - at least one main heading
  - the meta description
- cover the main entities linked to the query:
  - companies
  - sectors
  - roles
  - countries
  - regulations
  - schools
  - funds
- add authoritative signals whenever relevant:
  - official sources
  - named institutions
  - dated references
  - explicit methodology
- use internal links intentionally to connect:
  - services
  - job roles
  - salary pages
  - studies
  - references
  - schools
  - ecosystem pages
- use external links only when they support a fact, definition or regulation
- add descriptive alt text to each useful image or illustration
- keep mobile readability high:
  - short paragraphs
  - short lists
  - visible subheadings
  - no dense text walls
- favor evergreen angles when possible, then add fresh updates on top
- review and refresh important pages regularly when:
  - rankings drop
  - sources change
  - better internal links become available
  - search intent evolves
- for article pages in particular:
  - answer the main question immediately in the intro
  - build H2 sections around user questions, decisions or concrete use cases
  - add a short mini-FAQ at the end when it strengthens extractability
  - use short, quotable sentences that can stand alone in AI-generated answers
  - keep at least one concrete source, institution, regulation or dated reference visible

## Preferred Content Formats

### 1. Job Role Pages

Target title examples:
- "Sales Developer EMEA : salaire, rôle, compétences en 2026"
- "Vétérinaire imageur : salaire, missions et compétences en France"

Required structure:
- direct answer: what is this role?
- responsibilities
- required skills
- salary range
- market demand / shortage level
- why companies hire this role
- CTA

### 2. Salary Pages

Target title examples:
- "Salaire biotech France 2026 : guide complet"
- "Salaire sales medtech France : repères 2026"

Required structure:
- direct answer
- average range
- by experience
- by role
- by sector
- by company stage when relevant (Seed / Série A / Série B / scale-up)
- trusted sources
- CTA

### 3. Market Insight Pages

Target title examples:
- "Pourquoi les entreprises biotech peinent à recruter en 2026"
- "Pourquoi la médecine nucléaire devient un sujet stratégique en France"

Required structure:
- direct answer
- problem explained clearly
- data points
- real business impact
- talent implications
- solutions
- CTA

### 3bis. Article GEO / SEO Pages

Target title examples:
- "Comment réduire le taux d’absentéisme dans les Life Sciences"
- "Index égalité femmes-hommes 2026 : ce que doivent faire les employeurs"

Required structure:
- direct answer in the intro
- one main idea per section
- question-based H2 titles
- short sourced proof blocks
- 2 to 4 internal links in the body
- mini-FAQ
- final action block or CTA

### 4. Comparison Pages

Target title examples:
- "Cabinet de recrutement biotech vs cabinet généraliste"
- "Recrutement internalisé vs cabinet spécialisé Life Sciences"

Required structure:
- direct answer
- key differences
- pros and cons
- when to choose each
- business impact
- CTA

## Style

- professional
- direct
- credible
- executive
- concise
- useful before being persuasive

Do not write like a startup landing page.

## SKS-Specific Content Logic

Each important page should connect at least two of these layers:
- market signal
- hiring implication
- salary implication
- ecosystem / school / fund / event angle
- decision-maker implication

The strongest pages on SKS TALENTS usually connect:
- roles
- salaries
- sectors
- funding or ecosystem dynamics
- conversion path to call or contact

Prioritize:
- official institutions
- official company websites
- France Biotech
- Business France
- Bpifrance / Le Hub
- LEEM
- SIDIV
- Centre Oscar Lambret
- Institut Pasteur de Dakar
- recognized market studies already cited on the site
- validated salary sources already used by SKS TALENTS pages

If a salary or fact is uncertain:
- say so
- narrow the claim
- do not invent precision

## Page UX Rules

- include a direct answer block early
- use cards, bullets and short sections
- make the CTA visible but not aggressive
- keep the premium consulting tone already used on the site
- preserve the current dark presentation language and search-driven navigation strategy
- make every page easy to understand in less than 30 seconds on mobile
- ensure the meta description is specific, useful and click-worthy, not generic

## CTA Rules

- Keep one dominant CTA goal per page.
- Use first-person CTA copy whenever the page is meant to convert directly.
- Put proof near the CTA, not only in the middle of the page.
- If the page is decision-oriented, make the reader feel:
  - what is at stake
  - what changes after action
  - why SKS is credible to help

Every GEO page must end with a soft business CTA:
- "Discuter de vos besoins"
- "Être rappelé"
- "Réserver un call"
- "Parler de votre recrutement"

Use Calendly or contact CTA depending on qualification level.
