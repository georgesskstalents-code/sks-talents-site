# Monthly Editorial Library

## Objective

Turn `skstalents.fr` into a reference library for Life Sciences, Animal Health, Diagnostic, MedTech, Petfood and adjacent AI/Cyber roles.

The monthly target is:

- `20` new job-role pages
- `8` to `12` supporting articles
- `1` benchmark or study refresh when relevant

## Job-role batch logic

Each monthly batch should prioritize:

1. Shortage roles with active demand
2. Strategic roles that influence scale, quality, revenue or compliance
3. Operational roles from R&D to commercialization
4. AI and cybersecurity roles adapted to health, diagnostics and animal health

## Mandatory structure for each job-role page

Each fiche métier must include:

1. H1 with exact job title
2. Short definition of the role
3. Missions and responsibilities
4. Skills required
5. Recommended studies and training
6. Potential schools
7. Typical career path
8. Salary benchmark
9. Market demand / shortage context
10. CTA toward contact, orientation or callback

## Mandatory structure for articles

Every supporting article should include:

1. Search intent
2. Market context
3. What companies are struggling with
4. Roles most affected
5. Salary / hiring / skill implications
6. Recommended actions
7. Internal links to job roles, schools, services and references

## Source lock for 2026 content

For all fund-related content produced through the end of 2026:

- use the France Biotech PDF `Cartographie des fonds d’investissement français en santé en 2024` as a trusted baseline
- keep figures exact when they are taken from the PDF
- name France Biotech explicitly in the copy when using those figures
- do not extrapolate beyond the PDF without a second verified source

For veterinary education and exercise content:

- use `veterinaire.fr` as the primary official source
- keep the wording exact when citing the number of schools, the role of the Ordre, or the official entry points for students and young graduates
- link back to the official pages for conditions of exercise, the annuaire and the schools

## SEO rules

- Avoid duplicate angles within the same month
- One primary keyword per fiche
- Use internal links to `/services`, `/contact`, `/references`, `/schools`, `/investment-funds`
- Keep titles explicit and human
- Prefer specific search patterns such as:
  - `salaire regulatory affairs biotech`
  - `fiche métier bioinformaticien ngs`
  - `comment devenir application specialist diagnostic`

## Quality guardrails

- Do not invent hiring numbers for specific companies without proof
- Separate verified facts from editorial interpretation
- Cite source names in the page copy when relevant
- Prefer shortage + usefulness + relevance over pure volume
- Refuse publication or sync when `source_name` / `source_url` are missing or do not belong to the approved source registry

## Monthly output expectation

By the end of each month, the site should gain a coherent content cluster:

- 20 job-role pages across sectors
- 8 to 12 articles linked to those roles
- stronger internal linking between jobs, schools, services and studies
