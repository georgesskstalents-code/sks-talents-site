# SKS TALENTS - Canonical Job Scopes

Date: 2026-04-24

This document is the canonical reference for the markets and functions covered by SKS TALENTS.

Rule:

- always keep `HR / CODIR roles (CEO, COO, CPO)` at the top of each scope when presenting the function coverage
- do not change these scopes unless explicitly requested

## 01. Biotech

Functions covered:

- HR / CODIR roles (CEO, COO, CPO)
- Production / Manufacturing
- Commercial / Business / Marketing
- Back office / Business support

## 02. Diagnostic

Sub-scope:

- IVD
- NGS

Functions covered:

- HR / CODIR roles (CEO, COO, CPO)
- Production / Manufacturing
- Commercial / Business / Marketing
- Back office / Business support

## 03. Deeptech applique Life Sciences

Functions covered:

- HR / CODIR roles (CEO, COO, CPO)
- Production / Manufacturing
- Commercial / Business / Marketing
- Back office / Business support

## 04. E-sante / Robotique

Sub-scope:

- E-sante
- Robotique

Functions covered:

- HR / CODIR roles (CEO, COO, CPO)
- R&D Manager
- Production
- Sales
- Marketing
- Support functions

## Presentation rule

When SKS TALENTS market coverage is presented, use this exact order:

1. Biotech
2. Diagnostic (IVD / NGS)
3. Deeptech applique Life Sciences
4. E-sante / Robotique

And for functions:

- always put `HR / CODIR roles (CEO, COO, CPO)` first
- then list the other functions for the scope
